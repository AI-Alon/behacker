📖 Explanation

Dangling DNS Record

CNAME pointing to an unclaimed external service

A dangling DNS record points to a resource (cloud storage bucket, CDN origin, SaaS endpoint) that has been deleted but whose DNS record was never removed. Find and claim these orphaned records to serve content under the legitimate domain.
