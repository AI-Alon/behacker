QR Code Redirect (QRishing)

Social Engineering — Malicious QR Codes

QR codes printed in physical locations (menus, flyers, parking meters) can be overlaid with malicious stickers. Victims scan what appears to be a legitimate QR code but are redirected to a phishing page. Unlike URLs, QR codes are not human-readable.
